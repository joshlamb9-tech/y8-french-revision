Welcome to Your Revision Hub

This site contains everything you need to revise for the Common Entrance French examinations. Each topic page includes key vocabulary, sentence builders, and practice activities.

Understanding the Levels

Both exams are 13+ Common Entrance. Year 8 students sit EITHER Level 1 OR Level 2 depending on their ability:

Level 1 Foundation 13+ CE - Essential vocabulary and basic grammar

Level 2 Higher 13+ CE - Everything from Level 1 PLUS extended vocabulary and more complex grammar

Both Levels Required for both exam levels

Check which level you're sitting, then look for the badges throughout the site to know what you need to learn!

How to Use This Site

  • Topic Pages: Click on any topic below to access vocabulary, examples, and practice activities
  • Grammar Reference: Review all required grammar points with clear explanations and examples
  • Knowledge Checklist: Track your progress and identify areas that need more work
  • Sentence Builders: Practice forming sentences using the structures you've learned
